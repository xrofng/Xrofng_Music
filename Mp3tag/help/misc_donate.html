Donations

Mp3tag is Freeware — Please consider donating!

I really like the idea of Mp3tag being free and I have no intention to commercialize Mp3tag.
 
Unfortunately, its hosting and development are not cost-free for me. There is a lot of traffic on the Mp3tag servers and the software I'm using for developing Mp3tag isn't free.
 
So, I am asking you to download my software, try it, and if you like it and can spare a few bucks, please come back and make a donation. Any amount is appreciated!
It is absolutely voluntary — so you won't get less support or crippled versions if you don't.

Thank you!
